Welcome

Raynor has worked over 30 years in the IT industry. Starting out as an electrical engineer for a small engineering firm, Ray transferred into the IT field in the mid 90’s after it became clear the internet was here to stay. When his firm decided to start moving their engineers from designing blueprints by hand to designing in engineering software, he was picked to head their IT department and add computer science to his electrical engineering degree.
